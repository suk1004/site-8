since 1971

Unveiling Vivienne Westwood's Evolution and Impact on Fashion.

A Journey of Iconic Fashion and Timeless Rebellion

Vivienne Westwood, a trailblazing brand since 1971, has marked the fashion world with iconic styles. From the "Let It Rock" beginnings to challenging norms at the "SEX" boutique in the 1970s, and venturing into the New Romantic movement in the 1980s, the brand established itself as a trendsetter.


The 1990s emphasized activism, blending it with high fashion in the "Vivienne Westwood Red Label" and "Anglomania" collections. In the 2000s, the brand led sustainability efforts through projects like "Ethical Fashion Africa."

  • Today, Vivienne Westwood stands globally as a symbol of timeless elegance and rebellion, with an unwavering commitment to sustainability and creativity.


    The brand's vision extends beyond fashion, valuing individuality, rebellion, and environmental consciousness, inspiring positive change worldwide. Join us in walking the path of innovation, expressing ourselves while carrying Vivienne Westwood's legacy into the future.

Sustainability and Innovation

Emphasized eco-friendly fashion concepts and embraced the influence of the digital revolution, offering unique styles that resonate with the masses.

Diverse Experiments and Radicalism

Emergence of the grunge style and experimental use of colorful and diverse patterns with societal satire and critical messages.

Noble and Glamorous Style

Showcased a style reflecting royal inspirations with elaborate designs and a variety of colors.
